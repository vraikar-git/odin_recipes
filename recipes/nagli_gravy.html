Nagli(Lady) Fish Gravy

Description

A typical goan konkani gravy recipe made with healty adn tasty Nagli(Lady) Fish

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Nagli(Kane) fish
  • 2 Onion (Finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander seeds
  • 4 Red chillies (Byadgi or Kashmiri)
  • 1 cup Coconut (Grated)
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • Tamarind (Marble size)
  • 1 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Clean and wash the fish well and keep aside.
  2. To prepare the masala paste, heat a tadka pan and add 2 tsp coconut oil, 1/2 tsp coriander seeds and 4 broken red chillies. Fry until the coriander seeds turn aromatic and slighlty brown in colour.
  3. Transfer the above fried mixture into a mixie jar and add 1 cup grated coconut, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, marble size tamarind and grind to a smooth paste by adding 1/2 a cup of water. Adjust water quantity while grinding.
  4. Transfer the ground paste into a bowl.
  5. Heat a thick bottomed pot and add 1.5 tbsp coconut oil.
  6. Add 2 finely chopped onion and fry until golden brown on medium low heat.
  7. Now, add the ground masala and 1 cup reserved water. Mix well.
  8. Add 1 tsp salt and mix well. Adjust salt according to the taste.
  9. Close the lid and boil for 10 minutes. Make sure to keep stirring between intervals otherwise the masala may stick to the bottom of the pan.
  10. When the raw smell of the masala disappears place fish pieces one by one in the curry.
  11. Gently shake the pot to submerge fish pieces in the curry. Do not use spatula to mix because it will break the fish.
  12. Boil for 5 minutes exactly. At this point fish colour changes from transparent to white. Turn off the heat.
  13. Let the fish soak in all the spices. Serve after half hour.
